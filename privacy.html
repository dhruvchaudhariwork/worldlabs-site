1. Who we are & scope

World Labs (“we,” “us”) operates an expert evaluation service for AI-generated games: vetted game developers play generated games and submit structured judgments, which power a public benchmark and licensed research datasets. This policy covers this website and the sign-up flows on it. Contact: [ privacy@tryworldlabs.com, TBD ].

2. Information we collect

  • Waitlist sign-ups: your email address, so we can notify you when benchmark results publish.
  • Panel applications: name, email, professional background, specialty, and shipped-title credits you choose to share.
  • Business inquiries: contact details and the contents of your message.
  • Usage data: standard device and log data from visiting this site.

3. How we use information

  • To send benchmark results and product updates you asked for.
  • To review panel applications and, if accepted, onboard you as a panelist.
  • To respond to inquiries about evaluations and data licensing.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the site.

4. Expert judgments & published data

Evaluation data produced by panelists (rubric scores, critiques, pairwise choices) may be published in benchmark results and licensed as research datasets. Published and licensed data is attributed only to a panelist's specialty and years of experience, never to a name or email address.
