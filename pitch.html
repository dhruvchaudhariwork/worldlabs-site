COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE

Cross-agent intelligence is the moat

Every standalone tool operates in a silo. OpsFlow connects signals across support, subscriptions, inventory, and disputes to make smarter decisions.

Support → Retention
"Cancel my subscription" email triggers a save flow before the customer reaches the cancel page. Nobody else connects these.
Inventory → Support
When a SKU hits 3-day supply, SupportAgent automatically adds "ships in 5-7 days" to WISMO replies for that product. Context-aware.
Support → Disputes
Customer support transcripts automatically feed into dispute evidence. Disputely gets richer evidence = higher win rate.
