SupportAgent Autonomous

Monitors your support inbox, classifies intent, drafts replies using your brand voice, and auto-sends when confidence is high. Escalates edge cases with full context.

Setup

  1. Connect your Gmail support inbox via OAuth (read + send permissions)
  2. SupportAgent samples your last 20 replies to learn your brand voice and tone
  3. Set your auto-send confidence threshold (default: 90%)
  4. Choose which intents auto-reply vs. queue for human approval

Supported intents

  • WISMO — Where is my order?
  • CANCEL — Cancel order or subscription
  • REFUND — Refund request
  • ADDRESS — Update shipping address
  • PRODUCT — Product questions
  • OTHER — Unclassified (always escalated)

Auto-send vs. queue

Tickets classified with confidence above your threshold are auto-sent. Everything else goes to your Slack channel and dashboard for review. You can approve, edit, or reject any draft.

During the first 48 hours (shadow mode), all drafts are queued for approval regardless of confidence score. This lets you calibrate the agent before it goes autonomous.

RetentionAgent Subscription Engine

Full subscription management — billing, payment processing, failed payment recovery, and churn prevention. PCI DSS Level 1 compliant.

Setup

  1. Connect your Shopify store (if not already connected)
  2. Connect your payment processor (Stripe, or existing ReCharge/Loop)
  3. Configure subscription plans (pricing, frequency, trial periods)
  4. Set failed payment retry rules (default: 3 retries over 7 days with cascade timing)

Failed payment recovery

When a rebill declines, RetentionAgent runs a smart retry sequence:

  • Retry #1: 24 hours later, same processor
  • Retry #2: 72 hours later, alternate MID if available
  • Retry #3: 7 days later, with updated card info if customer responds to dunning email

Average recovery rate: 60-70% of failed payments.

Churn prevention

RetentionAgent detects churn signals across the platform:

  • Cancel intent detected in support emails (via SupportAgent)
  • Repeated skips or order pauses
  • Multiple support tickets in a short window
  • Failed payment after 3+ retries

When a signal fires, RetentionAgent triggers a save flow: pause offer, discount code, product swap, or personal outreach — configured by you.

InventoryAgent Scheduled

Computes days-of-cover for every SKU, alerts on stock-outs, and drafts PO emails to suppliers. Generates promo copy when inventory is heavy.

Setup

  1. Connect your Shopify store (inventory levels are pulled automatically)
  2. Set your thresholds: critical (<7 days default), low (<14 days), overstock (>45 days)
  3. Add supplier email addresses for auto-PO drafts

How it runs

days_of_cover = on_hand_qty / avg_daily_sales (30-day rolling)

InventoryAgent runs every 6 hours. When a SKU crosses a threshold:

  • Critical / Low: Slack alert + auto-drafted PO email to supplier
  • Overstock: AI generates promo copy (email subject lines, discount suggestions) to move excess inventory
Sales velocity is computed from your Shopify order history. The more history available, the more accurate the forecast. We recommend at least 30 days of order data.

Chargeback Protection Powered by Disputely

Real-time chargeback prevention and recovery through our integration with Disputely. Taps into Visa RDR, Ethoca, and CDRN alert networks.

How it works

  1. Customer files a dispute with their bank
  2. Card network fires an alert to Disputely within minutes
  3. Disputely auto-refunds to deflect the chargeback before it hits your ratio
  4. If a dispute escalates, Disputely compiles evidence and submits a response
  5. All activity surfaces in the OpsFlow dashboard

What OpsFlow adds

Because OpsFlow already holds your support transcripts, order data, and fulfillment timeline, Disputely gets richer evidence than it would standalone. Support conversations that prove the customer received the product are automatically included in dispute evidence.

Disputely is billed separately on their pay-per-save model. You only pay when they successfully deflect or recover a chargeback.

True Profit Dashboard

See your real profit — not just revenue. Connect your ad accounts, set COGS per SKU, and OpsFlow calculates true margin per order, per product, per channel.

Data sources

  • Revenue: Shopify orders (automatic)
  • Ad spend: Meta Ads, TikTok Ads, Google Ads (OAuth connect)
  • COGS: Set per SKU in the dashboard or bulk import via CSV
  • Shipping: Pulled from Shopify fulfillment data
  • Transaction fees: Calculated from payment processor rates
  • Chargebacks / returns: Pulled from Disputely + Shopify refund data

Metrics

true_profit = revenue - ad_spend - cogs - shipping - fees - returns - chargebacks

Available at the store level, per-channel (Meta vs TikTok vs Google vs Organic), per-product, and per-order. Real-time, updated with every new order.

Shopify Store Connection

Connect your Shopify store in under 5 minutes. OpsFlow needs three things from you.

1

Store URL

Enter your Shopify store URL (yourstore.myshopify.com). Find it in Shopify Admin → Settings → Domains.

2

Theme Access Password

Install the official Shopify Theme Access app from the Shopify App Store. Create a password for OpsFlow — this lets StorefrontAgent view and modify your theme code securely.

3

Admin API Access Token

In Settings → Apps and sales channels → Develop apps, create a new app called "OpsFlow." Configure the following API scopes:

read_products    write_products
read_orders      read_inventory
read_customers   read_fulfillments

Install the app and copy the Admin API access token. Paste it into the OpsFlow setup form.

Your store is safe. OpsFlow creates a duplicate theme for AI editing — your published store remains untouched. We cannot access your financial data, analytics, or staff accounts. You have full control over what gets published.

Security & Privacy

Data access

  • OpsFlow accesses only: theme code, products, orders, inventory, customers, and fulfillments
  • We cannot view or modify your finances, analytics, staff accounts, or billing
  • All theme changes are made to a duplicate — your live store stays safe
  • Gmail access is scoped to read + send on your support inbox only

Infrastructure

  • All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • API tokens stored in encrypted vault, never logged
  • Subscription billing is PCI DSS Level 1 compliant
  • SOC 2 Type II in progress

Data retention

  • Order and customer data retained for 90 days after disconnection
  • Full deletion available on request
  • GDPR-compliant data handling
