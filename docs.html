Shopify Store Connection

Three things to connect: your store URL, a theme access password, and an API token. The whole process takes about five minutes.

1

Store URL

Your Shopify store URL — find it in Settings → Domains. Looks like yourstore.myshopify.com.

2

Theme Access Password

Install the Shopify Theme Access app from the App Store and generate a password. This lets Forge read and modify your theme safely.

3

Admin API Token

In Settings → Apps → Develop apps, create a new app called "Optonomous" with the scopes below, then install and copy the access token.

ScopePurpose
read_products write_productsStorefront generation and catalog management
read_ordersSupport, profitability, and inventory signals
read_inventoryStock level monitoring and forecasting
read_customersSubscription management and churn analysis
read_fulfillmentsDelivery tracking for support and disputes
Your store is safe. Optonomous edits a duplicate theme — your published store is never touched. We cannot access finances, analytics, or staff accounts.

Resolve

Monitors your support inbox, classifies customer intent, drafts replies in your brand voice, and auto-sends when confidence is high.

Setup

Connect your Gmail support inbox via OAuth. Resolve samples your last 20 replies to learn brand voice, then you set an auto-send confidence threshold (default 90%) and choose which intents auto-reply versus queue for your review.

Supported intents

WISMO
Where is my order?
CANCEL
Cancel order or sub
REFUND
Refund request
ADDRESS
Update shipping
PRODUCT
Product questions
OTHER
Always escalated

Auto-send vs. queue

Tickets above your confidence threshold are sent automatically. Everything else goes to Slack and the dashboard for review — you can approve, edit, or reject any draft before it's sent.

Shadow mode. For the first 48 hours, all drafts are queued regardless of confidence. This lets you calibrate before going fully autonomous.

Pulse

Full subscription engine — billing, payment processing, failed payment recovery, and churn prevention. PCI DSS Level 1 compliant.

Setup

Connect your Shopify store and payment processor (Stripe, or existing ReCharge/Loop). Configure subscription plans — pricing, frequency, trial periods — and set your failed payment retry rules.

Failed payment recovery

When a payment fails, Pulse retries automatically across a 7-day window. Average recovery rate: 60–70%.

+24h
Same processor
+72h
Alternate MID
+7d
Updated card via dunning

Churn prevention

Pulse detects churn signals across the entire platform — cancel intent in support emails (via Resolve), repeated skips or pauses, multiple tickets in a short window, or payments failing after retries. When a signal fires, a save flow triggers automatically: pause offer, discount, product swap, or personal outreach.

Supply

Computes days-of-cover for every SKU, alerts on stock-outs before they happen, and drafts purchase orders automatically.

days_of_cover = on_hand_qty ÷ avg_daily_sales (30d rolling)

Runs every 6 hours, checking every SKU against your thresholds

Thresholds

Critical <7 days

Slack alert + auto-drafted PO email to your supplier

Low <14 days

Dashboard warning + PO recommendation

Healthy 14–45 days

No action needed

Overstock >45 days

AI generates promo copy to move excess

Sales velocity is computed from Shopify order history. We recommend at least 30 days of data for accurate forecasting.

Chargeback Protection

Real-time chargeback prevention and recovery through Disputely. Taps into Visa RDR, Ethoca, and CDRN alert networks.

How it works

1

Customer files a dispute

The cardholder contacts their bank to dispute a charge.

2

Alert intercepted

Card network fires a real-time alert to Disputely before the chargeback is finalized.

3

Auto-deflection

Disputely auto-refunds qualifying disputes to prevent them from becoming chargebacks.

4

Evidence compiled

If it escalates, Disputely compiles evidence and submits. Because Optonomous holds your support transcripts, order data, and fulfillment timeline, the evidence package is richer than standalone.

Pay-per-save pricing. Disputely is billed separately — you only pay when they deflect or recover a chargeback. No monthly fee.

Ledger

See real profit — not just revenue. Connect ad accounts, set COGS, and get true margin per order, per product, per channel.

Data sources

Revenue Shopify orders — connected automatically
Ad Spend Meta, TikTok, Google Ads — via OAuth
COGS Set per SKU or bulk import via CSV
Shipping From Shopify fulfillment data
Fees Calculated from payment processor rates
Chargebacks From Disputely + Shopify refunds
true_profit = revenue − ad_spend − cogs − shipping − fees − returns − chargebacks

Available at store, channel, product, and order level. Updated in real-time.

Signal

Autonomous ad campaign management across Meta, TikTok, and Google. Monitors performance, adjusts budgets, and optimizes for true profit — not just ROAS.

Setup

Connect your ad accounts via OAuth (Meta Business, TikTok for Business, Google Ads). Signal begins monitoring immediately and enters observation mode for 48 hours before making any budget changes.

What Signal does

Budget Scaling

Auto-increases budget on ad sets exceeding your ROAS target

Pause Underperformers

Stops spend on campaigns where CPA exceeds your threshold

Cross-Channel Reallocation

Shifts budget from low-ROAS channels to high-ROAS ones

Profit-Adjusted Optimization

Uses Ledger's true margin data, not just revenue-based ROAS

Cross-agent intelligence

Signal doesn't optimize in a vacuum. It pulls margin data from Ledger (so a 4x ROAS product with 10% margins gets treated differently than a 2x ROAS product with 60% margins), subscriber LTV from Pulse (so it values subscription-converting ads higher), and inventory levels from Supply (so it doesn't scale spend on products about to stock out).

Observation mode. Signal watches for 48 hours before making any changes. All proposed adjustments are queued for your approval during this period.

Security & Privacy

Scoped Access

Limited to theme code, products, orders, inventory, customers, and fulfillments. We cannot view finances, analytics, or staff accounts.

Safe Editing

Theme changes happen on a duplicate. Your live store is never modified. Gmail is scoped to read + send on the support inbox only.

Encryption

TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest. API tokens stored in an encrypted vault and never logged.

Compliance

PCI DSS Level 1 compliant for subscription billing. SOC 2 Type II in progress. GDPR-compliant data handling.

Data Retention

90-day retention after disconnection. Full deletion on request at any time.

Transparency

All agent actions are logged in your dashboard. Full audit trail for every automated decision.
