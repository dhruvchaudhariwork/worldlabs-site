Shopify Store Connection

Connect your Shopify store in under 5 minutes. OpsFlow needs three things.

1

Store URL

Enter your Shopify store URL (yourstore.myshopify.com). Find it in Shopify Admin → Settings → Domains.

2

Theme Access Password

Install the official Shopify Theme Access app. Create a password for OpsFlow — this lets StorefrontAgent securely view and modify your theme code.

3

Admin API Access Token

In Settings → Apps → Develop apps, create a new app called "OpsFlow" and configure these scopes:

read_products    write_products
read_orders      read_inventory
read_customers   read_fulfillments

Install the app and copy the Admin API access token into the OpsFlow setup form.

Your store is safe. OpsFlow creates a duplicate theme for editing. Your published store remains untouched. We cannot access finances, analytics, or staff accounts.

StorefrontAgent

Build a conversion-optimized Shopify storefront from a product description. No code, no templates, no developers.

How it works

1

Describe your product

Tell OpsFlow what you sell, who your target customer is, and your brand vibe. One paragraph is enough.

2

AI analyzes your niche

StorefrontAgent benchmarks top-performing stores in your category — layout patterns, copy structures, pricing psychology, upsell mechanics.

3

Generate your storefront

Full store: homepage, product pages, bundles, upsell flows, checkout optimization, and trust elements.

4

Iterate with prompts

"Make the hero more urgent." "Add a comparison table." "Switch to dark mode." Each version improves on the last.

5

Go live

Publish to your OpsFlow-hosted domain or connect a custom domain.

What gets generated

  • Homepage with hero, social proof, and featured products
  • Product pages with optimized copy and imagery
  • Bundle and "save more" offers
  • Post-purchase upsell flows
  • Checkout optimization
  • Mobile-responsive layout
  • SEO meta tags and structured data

SupportAgent

Monitors your support inbox, classifies intent, drafts replies in your brand voice, and auto-sends when confidence is high.

Setup

  1. Connect your Gmail support inbox via OAuth
  2. SupportAgent samples your last 20 replies to learn brand voice
  3. Set your auto-send confidence threshold (default: 90%)
  4. Choose which intents auto-reply vs. queue for approval

Supported intents

WISMO      Where is my order?
CANCEL     Cancel order or subscription
REFUND     Refund request
ADDRESS    Update shipping address
PRODUCT    Product questions
OTHER      Unclassified (always escalated)

Auto-send vs. queue

Tickets above your confidence threshold are auto-sent. Everything else goes to Slack and the dashboard for review. You can approve, edit, or reject any draft.

Shadow mode. During the first 48 hours, all drafts are queued regardless of confidence. This lets you calibrate before going autonomous.

RetentionAgent

Full subscription management — billing, payment processing, failed payment recovery, and churn prevention. PCI DSS Level 1 compliant.

Setup

  1. Connect your Shopify store
  2. Connect your payment processor (Stripe, or existing ReCharge/Loop)
  3. Configure subscription plans (pricing, frequency, trials)
  4. Set failed payment retry rules (default: 3 retries over 7 days)

Failed payment recovery

Retry #1   24h later, same processor
Retry #2   72h later, alternate MID if available
Retry #3   7 days later, with updated card info from dunning email

Average recovery rate: 60-70% of failed payments.

Churn prevention

RetentionAgent detects churn signals across the platform:

  • Cancel intent in support emails (via SupportAgent cross-signal)
  • Repeated skips or order pauses
  • Multiple support tickets in a short window
  • Failed payment after 3+ retries

When a signal fires, a save flow triggers: pause offer, discount, product swap, or personal outreach.

InventoryAgent

Computes days-of-cover for every SKU, alerts on stock-outs, and drafts PO emails. Generates promo copy when inventory is heavy.

Setup

  1. Connect your Shopify store (inventory is pulled automatically)
  2. Set thresholds: critical (<7 days), low (<14 days), overstock (>45 days)
  3. Add supplier emails for auto-PO drafts

How it runs

days_of_cover = on_hand_qty / avg_daily_sales (30d rolling)

Runs every 6 hours. When a SKU crosses a threshold:

  • Critical / Low: Slack alert + auto-drafted PO email
  • Overstock: AI generates promo copy to move excess inventory
Sales velocity is computed from Shopify order history. We recommend at least 30 days of data for accurate forecasting.

Chargeback Protection

Real-time chargeback prevention and recovery through Disputely. Taps into Visa RDR, Ethoca, and CDRN alert networks.

How it works

  1. Customer files a dispute with their bank
  2. Card network fires an alert to Disputely
  3. Disputely auto-refunds to deflect the chargeback
  4. If it escalates, Disputely compiles evidence and submits
  5. All activity surfaces in the OpsFlow dashboard

OpsFlow advantage

Because OpsFlow holds your support transcripts, order data, and fulfillment timeline, Disputely gets richer evidence than standalone. Support conversations proving delivery are auto-included.

Disputely is billed separately on a pay-per-save model. You only pay when they deflect or recover a chargeback.

True Profit Dashboard

See real profit — not just revenue. Connect ad accounts, set COGS, and get true margin per order, per product, per channel.

Data sources

  • Revenue — Shopify orders (automatic)
  • Ad spend — Meta Ads, TikTok Ads, Google Ads (OAuth)
  • COGS — Set per SKU or bulk import via CSV
  • Shipping — From Shopify fulfillment data
  • Fees — Calculated from payment processor rates
  • Chargebacks / Returns — From Disputely + Shopify refunds

Formula

true_profit = revenue - ad_spend - cogs - shipping - fees - returns - chargebacks

Available at store, channel, product, and order level. Updated in real-time.

Security & Privacy

Data access

  • Access limited to: theme code, products, orders, inventory, customers, fulfillments
  • We cannot view finances, analytics, staff accounts, or billing
  • Theme changes made to a duplicate — live store stays safe
  • Gmail scoped to read + send on support inbox only

Infrastructure

  • Encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • API tokens stored in encrypted vault, never logged
  • PCI DSS Level 1 compliant (subscription billing)
  • SOC 2 Type II in progress

Data retention

  • 90-day retention after disconnection
  • Full deletion on request
  • GDPR-compliant handling
