HOW OPSFLOW WORKS

Three agents, one command center

Each module runs autonomously but reports to a single dashboard. Human-in-the-loop approval keeps you in control.

1

Ingest

Platform webhooks and Gmail watchers detect disputes, support emails, and inventory changes in real time. Events are routed through a Redis-backed queue to the right agent.

2

Reason

Specialized AI agents classify intent, compile evidence, compute inventory metrics, and draft responses using Claude 3.5 and GPT-4o with store-specific templates.

3

Act

Auto-send low-risk replies, push drafts to Slack for approval, generate PDF evidence bundles, and alert on inventory thresholds — all logged in an audit trail.

AGENT WORKFLOWS

Step by step, fully auditable

Each agent follows a deterministic pipeline with LLM reasoning at key decision points. Human approval gates prevent auto-action on sensitive tasks.

1

DisputeAgent

  • 01Platform dispute webhook or Gmail chargeback email triggers agent
  • 02Fetch order JSON, fulfillment events, tracking, customer comms
  • 03Compile evidence: timeline, tracking screenshots, T&C
  • 04LLM drafts reason-specific dispute response
  • 05Output: Markdown + PDF bundle + Slack approval
2

SupportAgent

  • 01New support email via Gmail push notification
  • 02Classify: WISMO, Cancel, Refund, Address Change, Other
  • 03Pull order data, tracking status, subscription state
  • 04LLM drafts reply with intent-specific template
  • 05Auto-send if confidence > threshold, else queue
3

InventoryAgent

  • 01Scheduled job runs every 6 hours via cron
  • 02Compute days_of_cover = on_hand / avg_daily_sales
  • 03<7 days: Slack alert + auto-drafted PO email
  • 04>45 days: LLM generates promo copy
  • 05Dashboard shows SKUs with recommended action

ONBOARDING

Live in 30 minutes, autonomous in 48 hours

Connect your store, review your templates, and let the agents shadow your team for 48 hours before going fully autonomous.

1

Connect — 5 min

OAuth into your e-commerce platform and Gmail inbox. OpsFlow installs as an app and pulls the last 90 days of orders, inventory levels, and active disputes.

2

Configure — 15 min

Set auto-send confidence thresholds, upload your T&C, choose which intents auto-reply vs. queue. OpsFlow generates reply templates matched to your brand voice.

3

Shadow — 48 hrs

All agents run in shadow mode. Every draft goes to Slack and the dashboard for your team to approve, edit, or reject. The system learns your preferences.

4

Go Live

Flip agents to autonomous mode. SupportAgent auto-sends high-confidence replies. DisputeAgent pushes drafts to Slack. InventoryAgent alerts on thresholds.

How AI cost works

80% of LLM calls use fast, cheap models (GPT-4o-mini at $0.15/1M tokens) for classification. Only complex drafts use larger models. Avg AI cost: ~$15/store/mo — built into your flat rate.

%

Unit economics

At $249/store/mo with ~$15 in AI costs and ~$5 in infra, gross margins are 85%+. Per-ticket models like Gorgias cost merchants more at scale — we stay flat.
