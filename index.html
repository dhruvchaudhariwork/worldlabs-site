HOW OPSFLOW WORKS

Tell us what you sell. We handle the rest.

From building your storefront to running day-to-day operations. Describe your product, and OpsFlow takes it from there.

1

Build

Describe your product. StorefrontAgent analyzes top stores in your niche and generates a conversion-optimized storefront — product pages, bundles, upsells, checkout. Iterate with prompts.

2

Connect

Link your payment processor, support inbox, and supplier contacts. Agents pull in your order history, inventory levels, and customer data. 30 minutes of setup.

3

Autopilot

Agents go live: auto-reply support tickets, recover failed payments, alert on inventory, deflect chargebacks. You approve or override from one dashboard.

PLATFORM FEATURES

Your ops team, fully automated

Not another dashboard to check. Not another app to configure. Seven modules that do the work — from building your store to running it.

DISPUTES — POWERED BY DISPUTELY

Chargeback Protection

Integrated chargeback prevention and recovery powered by Disputely. Real-time alerts via Visa RDR, Ethoca, and CDRN networks intercept disputes before they hit your account. Auto-refunds, evidence compilation, and response submission — all surfaced in the OpsFlow dashboard.

Visa RDR Ethoca alerts Auto-deflection Disputely
SUPPORT

Support Autoresolver

Monitors your support inbox for common issues — WISMO, cancellations, address changes. Classifies intent, pulls relevant order data, and drafts empathetic replies. Auto-sends simple cases; escalates edge cases with full context.

Intent classification Auto-reply Smart escalation
INVENTORY

Inventory Alerts

Computes days-of-cover for every SKU. Flags stock-out risks (<7 days) with auto-drafted PO emails. Detects excess inventory (>45 days) and generates promo copy to move product.

Days-of-cover PO drafts Promo copy
SUBSCRIPTIONS

Subscription Engine

Full subscription management built in — setup, billing, payment processing, and support. We handle failed payment recovery with smart retry cascading and MID routing, plus churn prevention that detects cancel intent from support tickets and triggers save flows. Zero upfront cost to the merchant, PCI DSS Level 1 compliant. Designed to increase AOV and CLTV on autopilot.

Subscription billing Payment processing Failed rebill recovery Churn prevention PCI DSS L1
STOREFRONT

StorefrontAgent

Describe your product and the AI builds a conversion-optimized storefront — product pages, bundles, upsells, checkout flows. It analyzes top-performing stores in your niche and iterates with every prompt. No code, no templates, no developers. Hosted and managed by OpsFlow.

AI store builder Niche analysis Prompt-to-iterate Hosted
