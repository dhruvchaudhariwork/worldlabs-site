HOW IT WORKS

Tell us what you sell. We handle the rest.

From building your storefront to running day-to-day operations. Three steps.

1

Build

Describe your product. Forge analyzes top stores in your niche and generates a conversion-optimized storefront — product pages, bundles, upsells, checkout.

2

Connect

Link your payment processor, support inbox, and supplier contacts. Agents pull in your order history, inventory levels, and customer data.

3

Autopilot

Agents go live: auto-reply support tickets, recover failed payments, alert on inventory, deflect chargebacks. You approve or override from one dashboard.

PLATFORM

Your ops team, fully automated

STOREFRONT

Forge

Describe your product and the AI builds a conversion-optimized storefront — product pages, bundles, upsells, checkout flows. Iterate with prompts. Hosted and managed.

SUPPORT

Resolve

Monitors your inbox, classifies intent, drafts replies in your brand voice, and auto-sends high-confidence responses. Escalates edge cases with full context.

SUBSCRIPTIONS

Pulse

Full subscription management — billing, payment processing, failed rebill recovery with smart retry cascading, and churn prevention via save flows. PCI DSS Level 1.

INVENTORY

Supply

Computes days-of-cover for every SKU. Flags stock-out risks with auto-drafted PO emails. Generates promo copy when inventory is heavy.

DISPUTES — POWERED BY DISPUTELY

Chargeback Protection

Integrated prevention and recovery via Disputely. Real-time alerts from Visa RDR, Ethoca, and CDRN networks. Auto-deflection before chargebacks hit your ratio.

PROFITABILITY

Ledger

Connect Meta, TikTok, and Google ad accounts. Set COGS per SKU. See real profit per order, per product, per channel — not just revenue.
