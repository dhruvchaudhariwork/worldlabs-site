How a score is made

01

Build

A model is handed a spec and builds inside an instrumented game world.

02

Play

Panelists play the resulting build — the actual artifact, running.

03

Score

Each panelist scores the categories they're qualified to judge, with written reasoning.

04

Aggregate

Median across panelists per category; mean across a model's runs.
