1Mission

We are a commerce data research company. Our mission is to bring AI into the real economy through purchase behavior — the clearest signal of what people truly want.

2Process

We build purchase datasets with the same rigor researchers bring to models.

i.

Hypothesize

Identify a commerce capability we want to unlock — basket prediction, demand forecasting, return-risk.

ii.

Design

Architect the shape of transaction data needed to teach a model that capability.

iii.

Source

Aggregate consented, privacy-safe purchase signals at the right granularity.

iv.

Evaluate & Iterate

Measure data quality and tune the collection until a small, high-signal set is achieved.

v.

Productionize

Scale the dataset across millions of orders, SKUs, and merchants.

vi.

Release

Publish the dataset, and continuously refresh it over time.

Our datasets are used by leading retailers and AI labs that work with recommendation, demand forecasting, pricing, fraud, and agentic shopping.

3Featured Datasets

A dataset suite designed for recommendation, forecasting, and agentic commerce

Ledger

Our flagship U.S. dataset of itemized, basket-level purchases across thousands of merchants and categories.

Atlas

A multi-market dataset spanning 15+ countries, with currency, category, and channel metadata in the same format as Ledger.

Cohort

Longitudinal purchase histories that follow households over time. Built for retention, LTV, and repeat-purchase modeling.

Catalog

A structured product graph of SKUs, attributes, images, and pricing across domains and retailers.
