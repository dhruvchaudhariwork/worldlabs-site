Research

Game-native judgment,
shaped into datasets

We develop expert evaluation datasets with the rigor of a playtest lab: vetted game developers, blind sessions, anchored rubrics, and multi-rater agreement checks. Every judgment carries full provenance, from rater specialty to time played.

Featured datasets

A suite designed for world models, game agents, and generative game systems

PlaytestFlagship

Structured expert evals of whole AI-generated games. Each record is a blind, timed session by a shipped game developer: seven rubric dimensions scored against fixed anchors, a written critique, a ranked fix list, and session metadata including time played and broken-build flags.

Versus

Pairwise preference data built for reward models and RLHF. Two outputs from the same prompt, judged head-to-head by an expert: a winner, a confidence grade, and a written rationale. Runs on whole games, levels, quests, or any artifact in the suite.

Coach

Gameplay decision judgments from high-ranked players and coaches. AI play is rated move by move and across full trajectories: decision quality, the better alternative, the reason, and mistake severity. Built for game-playing agents and world models.

Cast

Extended-session evaluations of AI NPCs and in-game agents. Experts rate conversations and behavior on memory consistency, personality, improvisation, and immersion, with full dialogue transcripts attached to every judgment.
